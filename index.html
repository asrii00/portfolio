Pelifarmi - Game dev

Pelifarmi is a game industry oriented training program I completed in 2018. I learned about the industry standards and how games are developed, from creating a game concept to producing a playable demo, both in theory and in practice.

I worked mainly as the team's producer and took responsibility for the project's documentation and promotional materials as well as copywriting for the game itself.

Illustration and concept art

Example: Daily paintings for a personal project called "Phantasia Borealis".

Loosely inspired by Bethesda's Skyrim and ancient Finnish beliefs, Phantasia Borealis is a story set in a northern land that spirits and gods used to roam - and maybe they still do, though they haven't appeared since the famine began. A personal loss sends the protagonist on a journey across the continent and all the way into the depths of the Underworld.
